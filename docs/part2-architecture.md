# Part II: Architecture and Design

Part II covers the architecture and design aspects of infrastructure and platform management. These chapters guide you through designing infrastructure solutions that meet business requirements.

---

## Chapters in This Part

### [Chapter 4: Infrastructure Architecture Principles](/InfrastructureAndPlatformManagementHandbook/chapters/04-infrastructure-architecture/)
Core architecture principles, design patterns, reference architectures, and architectural decision-making frameworks.

### [Chapter 5: Cloud Platform Architecture](/InfrastructureAndPlatformManagementHandbook/chapters/05-cloud-architecture/)
Cloud deployment models, IaaS/PaaS/SaaS considerations, multi-cloud and hybrid cloud strategies, and cloud-native design patterns.

### [Chapter 6: Network and Security Architecture](/InfrastructureAndPlatformManagementHandbook/chapters/06-network-security/)
Network design principles, segmentation strategies, security controls, zero trust architecture, and encryption standards.

### [Chapter 7: High Availability and Disaster Recovery](/InfrastructureAndPlatformManagementHandbook/chapters/07-high-availability/)
Availability design patterns, redundancy strategies, disaster recovery planning, RTO/RPO definition, and business continuity.

---

## Learning Outcomes

After completing Part II, you will be able to:

- Apply infrastructure architecture principles to design decisions
- Evaluate and select appropriate cloud platforms and services
- Design secure, segmented network architectures
- Implement high availability and disaster recovery solutions
- Create infrastructure designs that meet business requirements
