# Part I: Foundations

Part I establishes the fundamental concepts and strategic framework for Infrastructure and Platform Management. These chapters provide the foundation upon which all subsequent infrastructure practices are built.

---

## Chapters in This Part

### [Chapter 1: Introduction to Infrastructure and Platform Management](/InfrastructureAndPlatformManagementHandbook/chapters/01-introduction/)
An introduction to the ITIL Infrastructure and Platform Management practice, its purpose, scope, and how it integrates with other ITSM practices.

### [Chapter 2: Core Concepts and Definitions](/InfrastructureAndPlatformManagementHandbook/chapters/02-core-concepts/)
Key terminology, infrastructure types, platform categories, and fundamental concepts including Infrastructure as Code.

### [Chapter 3: Strategic Framework and Critical Success Factors](/InfrastructureAndPlatformManagementHandbook/chapters/03-strategic-framework/)
The strategic framework for infrastructure management, 8 critical success factors, 6 key performance indicators, and the 5-level maturity model.

---

## Learning Outcomes

After completing Part I, you will be able to:

- Understand the purpose and scope of infrastructure and platform management
- Define key concepts and terminology used throughout the handbook
- Apply the strategic framework to your organization
- Identify critical success factors for infrastructure initiatives
- Assess organizational maturity and identify improvement areas
