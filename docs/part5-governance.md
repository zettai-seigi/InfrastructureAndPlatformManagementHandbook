# Part V: Governance and Controls

Part V covers governance, policies, and cost management for infrastructure. These chapters guide you through establishing governance frameworks and optimizing infrastructure costs.

---

## Chapters in This Part

### [Chapter 15: Governance Framework and Policies](/InfrastructureAndPlatformManagementHandbook/chapters/15-governance-framework/)
Infrastructure governance structure, policies and standards, compliance requirements, audit controls, and security governance.

### [Chapter 16: Cost Management and FinOps](/InfrastructureAndPlatformManagementHandbook/chapters/16-cost-management/)
FinOps principles, cost allocation and tagging, cloud cost optimization, reserved instances, and cost governance.

---

## Learning Outcomes

After completing Part V, you will be able to:

- Establish effective infrastructure governance frameworks
- Develop and enforce infrastructure policies and standards
- Meet compliance and audit requirements
- Implement FinOps practices for cost optimization
- Manage and optimize infrastructure costs effectively
